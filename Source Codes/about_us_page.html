Avanti is where elegance
becomes a part of your family tradition.

In the vibrant mosaic of London's culinary scene, a gem named Avanti stands out, offering a distinctive fusion of Mediterranean flavors that pirouette on the taste buds. Steering this culinary haven is Gabbi Khidyer, a visionary chef whose expedition from the charming kitchens of Italy, starting in 1983 in Siena, to explorations through Spain has molded the essence of Avanti.

Gabbi's culinary journey commenced in the picturesque landscapes of Italy, where he started cooking in the enchanting city of Siena in 1983. From those early days, he honed his skills, studying the intricate techniques of Italian cuisine and immersing himself in the rich traditions that have defined the country's culinary legacy for centuries.

Italy became Gabbi's canvas, and he painted his culinary masterpiece across the diverse regions of the country. From the aromatic kitchens of Naples, where he perfected the art of crafting the ideal pizza crust, to the sun-soaked vineyards of Tuscany, where he learned the delicate balance of flavors in Tuscan cuisine, Gabbi's culinary education was a symphony of experiences. Each city, each town left its imprint on his palate, creating a mosaic of tastes that would later find a home in Avanti's menu.

His culinary sojourn extended beyond Italy, as Gabbi explored the vibrant gastronomy of Spain. The streets of Barcelona and the tapas bars of Seville became classrooms, each dish a lesson in the rich tapestry of Spanish flavors. Gabbi's travels infused Avanti with a new dimension as he incorporated authentic Spanish tapas into the menu, bringing the spirit of Spain to the heart of London.

In 2006, Gabbi brought his rich culinary tapestry to the bustling streets of London. Armed with a determination to share the authentic flavours of the Mediterranean, he set out to make a mark in the diverse and dynamic food landscape of the city. West London became his canvas, and Gabbi, the culinary artist, opened various successful restaurants, each one a testament to his commitment to quality and authenticity.

In 2018, Gabbi embarked on a new chapter in his culinary journey, unveiling the doors to Avanti in Chiswick. The restaurant was an instant success, attesting to the authenticity of its Italian and Spanish-inspired dishes and the warm, welcoming ambiance that enveloped diners. Avanti in Chiswick became a beloved establishment, a place where locals and visitors alike gathered to share laughter, stories, and, most importantly, exceptional food.

Buoyed by the success of the Chiswick branch, Gabbi's vision expanded, and he opened another Avanti in Fulham, offering a new community the opportunity to experience the magic of his culinary creations. The Fulham branch, like its predecessor, swiftly became a hub for food enthusiasts seeking a taste of the Mediterranean and Spanish tapas in the heart of London.

Never one to rest on his laurels, Gabbi's journey continued with the recent inauguration of Avanti in Acton. This newest addition to the Avanti family is a testament to Gabbi's unwavering commitment to growth, innovation, and the community. The Acton branch, like its siblings, captures the essence of Mediterranean dining, inviting guests on a journey through the diverse landscapes of Italy and Spain.

Beyond the exquisite dishes that grace Avanti's menu, Gabbi's commitment to community sets his establishments apart. Each restaurant becomes a gathering place, a hub where neighbors become friends, and strangers become part of the extended Avanti family. Gabbi, not just a chef but a community man, believes in the transformative power of food to bring people together, fostering connections, and creating lasting memories.

Avanti, under Gabbi Khidyer's guidance, is more than a restaurant; it is a celebration of culinary artistry, cultural diversity, and the joy of sharing exceptional food with those we hold dear. From the kitchens of Italy and Spain and now woven into the fabric of London, Gabbi's journey is a testament to the transformative power of passion, persistence, and the universal language of delectable cuisine. Avanti – a name that beckons you to move forward, savor the moment, and relish the extraordinary flavors of life.
